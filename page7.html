FAQ

Can I place an order without registering?

No, you have to register as a member to place an order. However, you can still browse products and check out the our website before you register. Registration is free and there is no obligation to shop after you have registered. Register now!

Can a receipt be issued?

The products sold on our website are all authentic and licensed products, and all self-operated products have a receipt.

Can the goods be inspected on the spot after payment?

After you sign for payment, you can check and accept the goods on the spot. If there is a problem with the product, please submit a return or exchange application in 55076600, and professional after-sales personnel will solve it for you.

Why does Air fryer food store use JavaScript, and is it safe?

We use JavaScript to enhance the pages you receive. For example, we use it to calculate the updated cost of your order every time you put a new item in your shopping cart. Without Javascript, our website would not load properly, and would be extremely difficult to use.
